Lost Farms

HMHG are very keen to find any old photographs (preferably before the mid-1980's) of the buildings on the moor. Photographs of these farms still standing would help our research tremendously! If you have any - please contact us. These are the ones we have found so far. A huge thank you to all those who have contributed.

Nook - courtesy of Laura Clifton circa 1905-1910. Marjorie is the tall girl in the middle and Norah is beside her, their mother Elizabeth is next in line.

Laura's Great Great Grandmother Elizabeth Hall is second from the right. The girl in the straw hat looks like Norah Hall, her daughter.

On the far left is John Howard Hall, who bought the house. His daughter Norah is beside him, and his other daughter Marjorie is in the centre at the back. Elizabeth Hall, their mother is on the chair in the dark dress. The rest are her relatives, the Deardens, with their spouses and children.
